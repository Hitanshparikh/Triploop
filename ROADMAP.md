# JourneyOS AI — Visionary Roadmap 2026-2027

Our mission is to move beyond simple logistics and solve the **emotional complexity** of travel.

## 🏁 Phase 1: The Core OS (COMPLETED)
*   **Magic Auto-Fill™**: Real-time NLP parsing of human travel intent.
*   **Hyper-Discovery Grid**: 5ms latency discovery engine for global hotspots.
*   **Mood-Adaptive Interface**: Design system that shifts color palettes and pacing based on user energy.
*   **Predictive Simulation V1**: Fatigue and budget variance modeling.

## 🚀 Phase 2: Hyper-Personalization (Q3 2026)
*   **AR Vision Integration**: "Mirror View" in-browser AR that allows users to virtually "walk" through Taipei or Paris before booking.
*   **Neural Expense Splitting**: Automatically detect and split bills via receipt scanning and AI.
*   **Biometric Mood-Sync**: Optional integration with wearable devices (Apple Watch/Fitbit) to adjust travel plans automatically when high stress is detected.

## 🌍 Phase 3: Global Ecosystem (Q1 2027)
*   **Decentralized Travel Ledger (DTL)**: Move all booking confirmations to a private blockchain to prevent overbooking and fraud.
*   **AI-Autonomous Negotiation Agent**: A background AI that negotiates prices with hotels and airlines in real-time on behalf of the user.
*   **Carbon-Negative Routing**: Proprietary algorithm to ensure every JourneyOS trip is 110% carbon offset via direct air capture integration.

## 💫 Phase 4: The Meta-Traveler (2028+)
*   **Digital Twin Simulation**: Simulate a 10-year travel history to provide "Wisdom Recommendations" based on your future self's hypothetical preferences.
